Working at Citizen Code, LLC

Over the last year I have been working on Agile teams, but this one was my first flat organization, based on the principles of Holocracy. Working here was all about making your best judgement and pushing constantly forward with that decision, this was expected in all areas of our culture and work. That moto kept us to the most basic principle of always making a product more sucessful through iterations. Focusing on the biggest impacts with a minimal amount of features to get that done. This took the position of highest priority and everything else was pushed aside.

As they say, "Make it fast, make it work, make it right."
